Frequently Asked Questions

How does the secure messaging work?
Messages are encrypted and can only be read once. After viewing, they are permanently deleted from our servers.
Why can't I access my message again?
This is by design for maximum security. Messages are automatically deleted after being read once to ensure complete privacy.
Do you store any personal information?
No, we don't collect, store, or track any personal information. See our Privacy Policy for details.
Is there a message size limit?
Yes, messages are limited to ensure optimal security and performance. If you need to send larger content, consider breaking it into multiple messages.
How long are messages stored?
Messages are stored temporarily until they are read once, after which they are immediately and permanently deleted.
Can I recover a deleted message?
No, once a message is read and deleted, it cannot be recovered. This is a security feature, not a limitation.
