‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How secure are my messages?
A: All messages are encrypted end-to-end using military-grade encryption. We cannot read your messages - only you and your recipient can decrypt them.
Q: Do you store my messages?
A: No. Messages are temporarily stored on our servers only during transit (usually seconds) and are automatically deleted once delivered. We have zero-knowledge architecture.
Q: Can you recover lost messages?
A: No. By design, we cannot recover messages because we don't have access to your encryption keys. This ensures maximum privacy and security.
Q: What data do you collect?
A: We collect no personal data, message content, or usage patterns. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Q: Is Safe Whisper free to use?
A: Yes, Safe Whisper is completely free to use. There are no premium features or subscription plans.
