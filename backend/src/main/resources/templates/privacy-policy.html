Privacy Policy

Simple Promise: We don't collect, store, or access any of your personal data or message content.

Last updated:

What We Don't Collect

Unlike most online services, our secure messaging platform is designed with privacy at its core:

  • No Personal Information: We don't ask for or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any other personal identifiers
  • No Message Content: Your messages are encrypted and automatically deleted after being read once
  • No Usage Tracking: We don't track your browsing behavior, IP addresses, or usage patterns
  • No Cookies: We don't use tracking cookies or any persistent storage on your device
  • No Analytics: We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party tracking services

How Our Service Works

Our secure messaging service operates on the principle of ephemeral communication:

  • Messages are encrypted before being stored temporarily on our servers
  • Messages are automatically deleted immediately after being read once
  • No permanent records are kept of any communications
  • Each message has a unique, one-time access link

Data Security

Even though we don't collect personal data, we still maintain strong security practices:

  • All communications are encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • Messages are encrypted at rest on our servers
  • Our servers are secured and regularly updated
  • We follow industry best practices for data security

Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party services that would have access to your data or messages. This includes:

  • No advertising networks
  • No analytics services
  • No social media integrations
  • No external APIs that could access your content

Technical Information

The only technical information we may temporarily process includes:

  • Basic server logs for security and operational purposes (automatically deleted)
  • Anonymous aggregate statistics (number of messages sent, but no content or user data)

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, traditional data rights (like data portability or deletion requests) don't apply. However:

  • You can use our service completely anonymously
  • You control when and how your messages are accessed
  • All messages are automatically deleted after reading

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this policy and the "Last updated" date above. Since we don't collect contact information, we cannot notify users directly of changes.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices, you can reach us through our support page.

Bottom Line: This service is designed to be as private as possible. We can't access your messages, we don't know who you are, and we don't track what you do.
